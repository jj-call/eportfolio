About me

Reflections


Collaborative learning discussions:
The discussions have been very beneficial in learning about security technologies and why investment in cyber security is essential in today’s global society. The feedback from peers has supported my learning, whether by adding value to my initial thoughts, clarifying areas of limited knowledge, and enhancing the quality of discussion from different perspectives. In the second discussion, I took on feedback from my tutor to increase the number of posts made. The challenge broadened my thinking on the topic and made me reevaluate my process of time management so that I could achieve the task well.

Codio:
The Codio assignments provided a practical, interactive way to learn about numerous languages and prepare for the python programming assignment. The tutorials were clear, understandable and the challenges were appropriate to test knowledge. Some topics in python took me longer to learn and complete. However, I did find difficulty as sometimes Codio did not function correctly with some challenges. Also, the learning did not always save correctly, and the assessment report suggested I had missed questions or did not complete them. This was frustrating as I contacted the helpdesk to support, who said they needed to fix the issue, and I felt my efforts could have been spent more on the content. There were no issues when considering the project assignment, and I could complete the learning to the best of my ability.

Written assignment:
The written assignment was very engaging and allowed the opportunity to apply the knowledge gained to a real-life context. The study increased motivation to learn and discover deeper understandings whilst gaining new skills such as UML modelling. I managed to keep to the word limit; however, this was a challenge as I felt I could have written more with plenty of areas to offer critical analysis. The assessment feedback received was very positive, particularly for critical analysis and evaluation with targets to improve the layout by placing diagrams in the body of the report rather than referring to appendices for readability.

Programming assignment:
The programming assignment provided my most prominent challenge to complete. However, the skills and understanding gained have provided a greater incentive to deep dive into Python programming further, which will form part of my action plan to improve in the future.

Network and Information Security Management

----------------------------------

Learning Outcomes

■ Identify and analyse security risks and vulnerabilities in IT network systems and determine appropriate methodologies, tools and techniques to manage and/or solve them.
■ Design and critically appraise computer programs and systems to produce solutions that help manage and audit risk and security issues.
■ Gather and synthesise information from multiple sources (including internet security alerts and warning sites) to aid in the systematic analysis of security breaches and issues.
■ Articulate the legal, social, ethical and professional issues faced by information security professionals.

----------------------------------
